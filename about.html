Our Story

Born from a vision to make quality healthcare accessible, Althaia Pharma began its journey with a commitment to healing. Our name, rooted in the Greek word for 'to heal,' is a constant reminder of our purpose. We have grown from a passionate startup into a trusted pharmaceutical leader, driven by science, integrity, and a deep sense of responsibility to the communities we serve.

Our Mission

To pioneer, develop, and deliver innovative, high-quality, and affordable pharmaceutical solutions. We are dedicated to addressing unmet medical needs and improving the health and well-being of communities across the globe through scientific excellence and a patient-first approach.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized and trusted leader in the pharmaceutical industry, earning the confidence of healthcare professionals and patients alike. We aim to be synonymous with innovation, quality, and unwavering ethical practices, shaping the future of healthcare for the better.

Meet Our Directors

Guided by experience, driven by passion.

Bhavesh Tiwari

Director

As I reflect on my journey in the pharmaceutical industry spanning over 18 years, I am filled with gratitude for the opportunities and experiences that have shaped my professional trajectory. From my humble beginnings as a Bachelor of Pharmacy graduate to my previous role as a seasoned pharmaceutical professional at Johnson & Johnson, every step has been a learning curve, enriching me with invaluable insights and expertise across diverse therapeutic domains.

My journey commenced at USV, where I cultivated foundational knowledge in diabetes and cardiology. Subsequently, my tenure at Pfizer broadened my horizons, focusing extensively on diabetes management. Transitioning to Bristol Myers Squibb further enriched my experience, allowing me to explore the realms of cardiology and oncology. Today, at Althaia Pharma, I am privileged to contribute across a spectrum of therapeutic areas, including diabetes, central nervous system disorders, orthopedics, and oncology.

As we embark on the next phase of our journey, I am confident in our collective ability to drive innovation, enhance patient outcomes, and foster collaborative partnerships. Let us continue to push boundaries, embrace challenges, and make a meaningful difference in the lives of millions worldwide.
