Advanced Nutritional Support for Complete Wellness

A comprehensive multivitamin supplement designed to support your body's essential needs and enhance overall well-being.

What is Al Multihenze?

Al Multihenze is the medication applied for providing extra nutrition to the body. It is useful in curing Megaloblastic anemia, by supplying the essential minerals and vitamins for the growth of large red blood cells.

This medicine is also beneficial for supplementing nutrients for fighting vitamin B12 deficiency in both young and old. Doctors prescribe this tablet for fighting peripheral neuropathy, mainly for diabetic and alcoholic people.

It contains antioxidants that prevent untimely aging of body cells, mainly the skin. It is beneficial in eliminating fatigue by providing supplementary nutrition to the body system. This medicine is also useful in improving concentration, memory power, and other cognitive abilities.
